🌳 Wood (木 Mù)

Wood types are characterized by growth, flexibility, and vision. Like a tree, they reach upward and outward, always seeking expansion and new horizons.

Personality: Creative, visionary, idealistic, compassionate, generous

Challenges: Can be indecisive, impatient, stubborn when stressed

Compatible with: Water (nurtures growth) and Fire (Wood feeds Fire)

🔥 Fire (火 Huǒ)

Fire types radiate warmth, passion, and charisma. They light up any room and naturally draw others toward them with their enthusiasm and energy.

Personality: Passionate, confident, charismatic, enthusiastic, courageous

Challenges: Can be impulsive, arrogant, quick-tempered

Compatible with: Wood (Wood feeds Fire) and Earth (Fire creates Earth from ash)

⛰️ Earth (土 Tǔ)

Earth types are the foundation — reliable, steady, and nurturing. They provide stability and security for everyone around them.

Personality: Reliable, patient, practical, loyal, nurturing

Challenges: Can be stubborn, over-cautious, self-sacrificing

Compatible with: Metal (Metal excavates Earth) and Fire (Fire creates Earth)

⚪ Metal (金 Jīn)

Metal types are defined by clarity, precision, and inner strength. Like polished metal, they reflect light with perfect clarity and have strong boundaries.

Personality: Determined, organized, logical, direct, fair-minded

Challenges: Can be rigid, overly critical, emotionally detached

Compatible with: Water (Metal condenses Water) and Earth (Earth generates Metal)

💧 Water (水 Shuǐ)

Water types are adaptable, wise, and go with the flow. They find the path of least resistance while still moving toward their destination.

Personality: Intelligent, diplomatic, intuitive, adaptable, wise

Challenges: Can be overly passive, indecisive, emotionally sensitive

Compatible with: Metal (Metal condenses Water) and Wood (Water nourishes Wood)
