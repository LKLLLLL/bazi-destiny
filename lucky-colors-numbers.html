BaZi Lucky Colors, Numbers & Directions

Align with your element's auspicious energy — discover your lucky colors, prosperity numbers, favorable directions, and best times of day

In Chinese metaphysics, your Day Master element doesn't just describe your personality — it also reveals which colors, numbers, directions, and times of day carry the most auspicious energy for you. Wearing your lucky color, sleeping in your favorable direction, or closing deals during your peak time can subtly shift outcomes in your favor.

How BaZi Lucky Energy Works

The Five Elements follow a natural cycle of generation (相生) and control (相克). Elements that generate your element enhance your energy. Elements that your element generates bring you satisfaction and output. And elements that control your element should be used with caution.

Your "lucky" elements are those that support or are supported by your Day Master. Your "unlucky" elements are those that over-control or reverse-control your Day Master.

How to Use Your Lucky Directions

In BaZi and Feng Shui, directions correspond to elements. Your favorable direction is where your element is strongest:

  • East (90°–135°): Wood energy — best for Wood types
  • South (157°–202°): Fire energy — best for Fire and Earth types
  • West (247°–292°): Metal energy — best for Metal types
  • North (337°–22°): Water energy — best for Water types

Practical Applications

  • Sleep direction: Point your head toward your favorable direction when sleeping
  • Desk placement: Face your lucky direction when working
  • Door placement: Your main door facing your lucky direction strengthens career luck
  • Property selection: Choose a home or office with your favorable direction as the facing direction

Element Conflicts: What to Avoid

Each element has natural conflicts. Understanding what to avoid is as important as knowing what to embrace:

  • Wood: Avoid too much Metal (scissors cutting trees) — avoid white/silver decor in your personal spaces
  • Fire: Avoid too much Water (water extinguishes fire) — avoid black/blue dominant rooms for important meetings
  • Earth: Avoid too much Wood (wood pierces earth) — avoid large green plants in the southwest area of your home
  • Metal: Avoid too much Fire (fire melts metal) — avoid dominant red/orange lighting in your office
  • Water: Avoid too much Earth (earth blocks water) — avoid excessive brown/yellow in the north area of your home
